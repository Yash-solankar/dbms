Our Story

Founded in 2000, ElectroTech Components has grown from a small local supplier to a global leader in electronic component manufacturing. Our journey has been driven by innovation, quality, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Today, we serve over 1,000 clients worldwide, providing components that power everything from consumer electronics to advanced medical devices and aerospace systems.
